Who is Debra Winger?

Debra Winger is an American actress. She is noted for her acting in movies like An Officer and a Gentleman (1982), Terms of Endearment (1983), Shadowlands (1993), and more.

Recently in 2021, she was seen in four episodes of Mr. Corman (TV Series) as Ruth Corman.

Debra Winger- Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

Her birthdate is 16 May 1955 and the place she was born is Cleveland Heights, Ohio, the US. The name of her mother was Ruth plus her father’s name was Robert.

Her mom was an office manager while her dad was a meatpacker.

Similarly, Debra’s ancestry is Ashkenazi Jewish and as per her academics, she graduated from California State University, Northridge.

Debra Winger- Professional Life, Career

Debra Winger has become a popular face of the acting world where she made several screen appearances.

Moreover, her TV credits are Wonder Woman (1976-77), Tattletales (1977), In Treatment (2010), The Ranch (2016-20), Patriot (2018), and more.
