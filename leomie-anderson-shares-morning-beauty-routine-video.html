Leomie Anderson shares her speedy morning beauty routine

August 17, 2024
blog

    Leomie Anderson has worked with some of the industry'sleading make-up artists through her modelling career, so it's no surprise shehas mastered her own daily beauty routine.

    The British model has created a video showcasing her morningmake-up routine for H&M, and it is surprisingly easy to follow.

    Rather than spending hours in front of the mirror, Leomiehas managed to cut down her daily beauty look to just a few minutes, utilising productsfrom the high street retailer's make-up collection.

    H&M Beauty: Leomie Anderson’s Morning Beauty Routine

    Leomie Anderson has showcased her morning make-up routine

    After moisturising her skin, Leomie explains that sheapplies primer before her base to help her make-up last all day.

    "We need some foundation of course, under my eyes I'mgoing to put on some concealer, cover up any bags," she explains.

    LEOMIE ANDERSON'S BEST STYLE HITS

    The 23-year-old then moved onto curling her eyelashes andapplying an eyeliner pencil, which she said makes her lashes look thicker.

    Next up? Mascara. "Mascara is one of the most importantsteps for your morning beauty routine I think," Leomie says.

    The model said mascara is one of the most important steps in her beauty routine

    "Now we're going to move on to my eyebrows, so we'regoing to brush them up and give them a little shape. I like to mix a little bitof the light brown and dark brown, fill in any gaps that you see in youreyebrows. Liquid brow gel to fix them in place. Not bad."

    The Victoria's Secret star then applies a cream blusher tothe apples of her cheeks with her fingers.

    "It makes me look more rosey and youthful, like I'vebeen running for the bus or something," Leomie explains, before finishingthe look with a slick of clear lip gloss.

    Leomie showed off a short clip of her tutorial on Instagramon Wednesday, telling fans: "My makeup tutorial with @hm is out! Visittheir site now and get that fresh, just ran for a bus beauty."
