Kim Kardashian, Kylie, and Kris Jenner Arrive at Maison Margiela's Paris Show

July 30, 2024
blog

The Kardashian-Jenners are making waves at Paris Couture Week.

Today, Kim Kardashian, Kylie Jenner, and matriarch Kris Jenner made a grand entrance at Maison Margiela’s show, in fabulous looks from the fashion house that perfectly embodied the women’s different styles.

Pierre Suu//Getty Images

Kris—the momager and brain behind the Kardashian-Jenner empire—dressed like the boss she is in a sleek, baggy black suit. The star of the outfit was a long tailored coat with bulbous sleeves, which Kris bunched up, revealing the sleeves of her crisp white collared shirt underneath, as well as a pair of scrunchy black leather gloves. She wore the suit jacket with matching baggy trousers, plus Margiela’s controversial It shoe, the Tabi. Kris accessorized with some sporty black sunglasses, large diamond studs, and a black velvet bag. She wore her signature bob and had on glowy makeup, including a glossy pink lip.

Pierre Suu//Getty Images

Skims mogul Kim opted for her go-to silhouette: a bodycon dress. The black floor-length piece was embellished with circular cutouts all over, and had barely-there spaghetti straps; its dramatic hourglass silhouette expertly cinched Kim at the waist. To add to the drama, the reality star layered on a massive silver choker necklace in an intricate design, which cascaded from her neck to her chest. She also wore a glossy, pointy black manicure to match her inky gown, and opted for full bombshell glam and a straight hairdo.

Getty Images

Kylie, meanwhile, went in the opposite direction with her look. The Khy founder—who has been on an ethereal style streak in Paris—chose a dreamy, semi-sheer silver sequin dress with a scoop neck and pretty, short sleeves. She paired it elegantly with gauzy gray gloves and chunky white doll-like pumps, plus gold jewelry by Messika. She wore bronzy makeup, including a brown lip, and had her hair styled in a side-parted wet ’do.

Pierre Suu//Getty Images
