Kylie Jenner regularly posts revealing looks on Instagram, and her latest outfit is no exception.

Prior to stepping out in West Hollywood on Saturday night, the makeup mogul posed for some snaps wearing the skimpy yet casual look. According to @kyliejennercloset, Kylie was styled by Jill Jacobs, and her outfit consisted of a V-cut zip-up hoodie and matching sweatpants from Sami Miro Vintage, which she paired with a cotton-knit bra top from Bottega Veneta.

Jenner accessorized the laid-back look with a mixed media mushroom shoulder bag by Louis Vuitton, a pair of Bottega Veneta's board woven leather sandals, and body chains by Ettika.

Bottega Veneta Cotton-blend Bra Top

Kylie's latest look comes after she dressed up as a sexy sailor to promote her forthcoming Kylie Cosmetics collection. The lip kit queen rocked a very low-cut, off-the-shoulder bikini top, which she wore with tiny blue sailor-inspired shorts. Finally, thigh-high black stockings completed the eye-catching look.

Kylie recently spent her 23rd birthday in Turks and Caicos with several family members and friends. E! reported that she was joined by daughter Stormi Webster, sisters Khloé Kardashian and Kendall Jenner, and BFF Anastasia Karanikolaou, as well as Tristan Thompson and Rob Kardashian, for the epic celebration.
