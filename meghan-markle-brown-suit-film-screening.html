Meghan Markle Perfects Nine-to-Five Dressing in an All-Brown Suit

July 31, 2024
blog

Duchess Meghan made a rare, stylish appearance at a film screening yesterday in Los Angeles.

In new photos shared by director Misan Harriman on Instagram, the Duchess of Sussex is seen sitting onstage at a panel discussion, which she moderated, about his new short film The After.

For the occasion, Meghan brought out her most elegant nine-to-five attire, which this time consisted of an all-brown suit look. The outfit was composed of a sleek double-breasted blazer with black buttons, which Meghan layered over a fitted turtleneck top and paired with breezy, high-waisted suit pants. To finish off the chic look, she chose a pair of black suede pointed-toe pumps, small gold hoop earrings, and a gold-and-diamond constellation ring by Logan Hollowell.

As always, the Archetypes podcast host kept her makeup look natural; she had her hair pulled back in a neat, low ponytail.
