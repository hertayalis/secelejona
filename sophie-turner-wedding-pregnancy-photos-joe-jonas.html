Sophie Turner Posts Rare Pregnancy Baby Bump &amp; Wedding Photos

June 10, 2024
blog

After keeping the majority of her pregnancy under wraps, Sophie Turner has started sharing previously unseen baby bump photos now that her daughter, Willa, has arrived.

"Felt like we needed some content on this page... enjoy these throwbacks," Turner captioned her Instagram post. As well as sharing a beautiful photo of her baby bump, Turner also threw it back to her wedding day. The actress shared a wedding snap taken with husband Joe Jonas at their Las Vegas nuptials in May 2019.

The couple can be seen posing with their wedding cake, which features the surname Jonas on top, and has a row of cupcakes surrounding it. Meanwhile, in another picture, Turner can be seen holding a lemur.

A source recently told Us Weekly that Turner and Jonas are already thinking about expanding their family. "Joe and Sophie are already trying to have another baby," the source revealed. "They are really excited to expand their family. Having the baby has made them really close and want to have a large family together."

Following Willa's birth, a source reportedly revealed to Entertainment Tonight, "Joe and Sophie welcomed a baby girl ... and are over the moon." The source continued, "The couple is already obsessed and can't stop gloating about their new addition. The couple is taking time to enjoy this special moment and have only shared the news and updates with family and friends. With the pandemic Joe and Sophie have been very cautious about who is around them and their little girl."
