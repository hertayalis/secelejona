AEW Selling CM Punk Trans Pride T-Shirt

July 22, 2024
blog

AEW is all about joining social causes, and they are not finished supporting the LGBTQ movement even after Pride Month is over. The company just dropped a t-shirt in support of trans people that CM Punk is proudly wearing.

Since AEW Collision’s inception, CM Punk has been the central figure of the Saturday night show. His return on June 17th marked a big moment for the show. His name still carries a lot of weight, and now that name value is going to support a worthwhile cause.

CM Punk and Ricky Starks had a showdown for Punk’s ‘Real’ AEW World Championship during the recent Saturday episode. Punk won that match, and he was also representing the trans community at the same time.

During his entrance, Punk sported a revamped version of his Best in the World shirt, proudly displaying the Transgender flag. This choice aligns with Punk’s well-known advocacy for the LGBTQ+ community. AEW has taken steps to support this cause by releasing the shirt for a limited duration.

The shirt is currently up for purchase on ShopAEW.com, with the entirety of the proceeds being directed towards the Trevor Project. You can check out the link here to grab one of your own.

This subject means a lot to CM Punk, and he is proud to help out. Hopefully, this will go on to assist a lot of people through this t-shirt in many ways.
