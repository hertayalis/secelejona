Anya Taylor-Joy Wears Monochromatic Outfit For Tiffany's Ad

July 21, 2024
blog

Anya Taylor-Joy has perfected Hollywood screen siren style, on and off the (virtual) red carpet.

On Tuesday, the Queen's Gambit star was photographed in New York City filming an ad for Tiffany and Co. alongside model Alton Mason, in a sleek all-black outfit. Taylor-Joy wore a long double-breasted coat over a sweetheart neckline bustier with a pair of wide-legged trousers and slingback pumps. She gave the ensemble an elegant finish with a classic red nail polish manicure and a gold necklace and multiple stacked rings (courtesy of Tiffany's, of course).

At one point during filming, the actress took off the coat, giving a better look at the perfectly fitted bustier and a silver bracelet slung on her wrist.

The result? An effortless Hollywood-bombshell-off-duty look.

Taylor-Joy, who is also the face of Viktor and Rolf's Flowerbomb perfume, told InStyle in December that thanks to a busy schedule switching on and off between different characters, she hasn't "had a lot of time to work out my personal style."

"I like to play dress up," she said. "It's really fun to build an outfit rather than being stuck in any particular thing. Just deciding, 'Okay, what kind of girl do I want to be today?'"

Whoever she chooses to be, rest assured that we'll be taking notes.
