Who is Katherine Schwarzenegger?

Katherine Eunice Schwarzenegger was born on December 13, 1989, in Los Angeles, California, U.S. She is the eldest daughter of Arnold Schwarzenegger and author Maria Shriver. Katherine belongs to American nationality while she has mixed ancestry of Austrian, Irish, and German.

Katherine was born under the birth sign of Sagittarius. Her mother Maria is the only daughter of politician Sargent Shriver and activist Eunice Kennedy Shriver.
