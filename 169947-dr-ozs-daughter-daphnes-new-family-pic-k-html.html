Dr. Oz's Daughter Daphne's New Family Pic with Kids and Husband after Birth of 4th Child Gigi

September 06, 2024
blog

Daphne Oz, the daughter of Dr. Oz, warmed the heart of her followers with a new photo that features her husband, and her four little kids, as they celebrate the summer.

Daphne Oz showed off her family-of-six in a photo post on Instagram. The TV personality dressed in a white polo, and navy-blue trousers, cradled her new baby Giovanna "Gigi" Ines.

Beside the mum-of-four, her husband, John Jovanovic, carried their 1-year-old daughter, Dominica Celine. In front of the couple stood their oldest children, Philomena, 5, and Jovan Jr., 4.

"Summer 2019, you were a winner," with a star emoji.

Dr. Oz commented on the post, writing, "Couldn't agree more," and other followers appreciated the candid and cute family photo. One person wrote, "Cute family," and another commented, "You have the most beautiful family."

In June, the TV star shared a similar photo of her kids and Dr. Oz, when he celebrated his 59th birthday. At the time, Daphne was still pregnant with Gigi; she delivered the little girl on August 14.

The "The Chew" host shared a photo on Instagram of her three older children cramping her bed space, as she held onto their little sister. In the accompanying caption, she wrote,

"Our beautiful girl is here. Giovanna Ines Jovanovic (Gigi!!) joined our family, Wednesday, Augusts 14, 2019, at 9:03 pm, and our hearts and hospital bed have never been so full."

The couple got married in August 2010 and had three separate wedding ceremonies. The first was a civil ceremony at the Manhattan Municipal Marriage Bureau and the second a Serbian service at a Serbian Church. The third took place at a Swedenborgian Church.

Daphne and John met during their undergraduate days at Princeton, after which he proceeded to obtain an MBA at the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania.

The dad-of-four works as an investment analyst and has a successful career. The couple supports each other in their career, but above their professional commitments, they put their family first and are wholly dedicated.
