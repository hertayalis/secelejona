Joe Prunty was named the head coach of the Great Britain men's national basketball team in June 2013 after an extensive interview process following Chris Finch's resignation. The choice was initially somewhat controversial as Prunty had little previous international coaching experience. Prunty's first campaign as GB head coach was summer 2013. Despite being without top players Luol Deng, Ben Gordon, Joel Freeland, and Pops Mensah-Bonsu, Prunty began his tenure with a 61–55 win over Puerto Rico in an International Test Match at The Copper Box Arena, London. The team went on to upset Israel in their first game of Eurobasket 2013, earning a dramatic overtime victory. However, Great Britain ended the group phase with a 2–3 record, not good enough to advance. Because of this result, UK Sport cut its funding for the team.

In 2006, he left San Antonio to become a Dallas Mavericks assistant coach, becoming a part of the staff of former Spurs assistant coach Avery Johnson. Prunty left the Mavericks for the Portland Trail Blazers in 2008 when Avery Johnson was fired and replaced with Rick Carlisle.

Joseph Michael Prunty (born February 12, 1969) is an American professional basketball coach who was most recently the lead assistant coach for the Phoenix Suns of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Previously, he served as the head coach of the Milwaukee Bucks during the 2017–2018 season. In 37 games of the Prunty era, the Bucks went 21-16 (.568), the fourth best win percentage in franchise history among 16 head coaches. At one point, Prunty owned the Milwaukee record for playoff wins for a head coach in their first season (3). Prunty also was the head coach of the Great Britain men's national basketball team from June 2013 until September 2017. Prunty is a three time NBA Champion as an assistant with the San Antonio Spurs (1999, 2003, 2005) and is a 2006 Western Conference Champion with the Dallas Mavericks. Prunty has spent 23 seasons in the NBA with 7 different organizations. He has been a part of a remarkable 17 playoff appearances and is renowned as one of the best teachers in the American professional game. Prunty has coached the likes of some of the all time greats including Tim Duncan, Dirk Nowitzki, and Giannis Antetokounmpo to name a few.
